The Problem

New Army recruiters entered one of the Army's most mission-critical roles without a fully standardized onboarding system or a reliable enterprise method to track training progress. That created uneven training execution, weak leader visibility, and avoidable risk inside the organization responsible for sustaining the all-volunteer Army.

The Solution

L.I.N.K., the Leader's Integration and Navigation Kit, is a comprehensive Power Platform solution that gives Army recruiting leaders real-time visibility over training status, certification progress, and point-of-need resource delivery.

The Scale

Built for a 10,000+ person recruiting enterprise responsible for ensuring the Army can continue filling its ranks through an all-volunteer force.

The Constraints

Delivered inside a GCC High environment using standard licensing, limited personnel, limited platform expertise, and the practical constraints of a large government enterprise.

The Outcome

4% estimated increase to total organizational production, supported by record-high new recruiter training completion, stronger training delivery, improved leader visibility, and a projected 10% first-year production lift for new recruiters.

My Role

Served as Functional Consultant and Power Platform Developer: defined the business problem, mapped the process, built the solution, connected the data, briefed stakeholders, and supported adoption across the force.
